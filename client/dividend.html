TIME Tokens
The total supply of   TIME tokens is 25,000,000,000.
  • 3,750,000,000 (15% of total supply) will be allocated for use in airdrops and for other marketing purposes.
  • 21,250,000,000 TIME tokens (85% of total supply) will be issued as a reward to the community that uses the site.
TIME tokens are awarded to the community based on each EOS bid or won on the site. The number of TIME tokens awarded for each EOS bid or won is divided into four phases as described in the table below:

Dividend Payout
  • Each day, the profits of the site are distributed as follows:
    • 20% is distributed to the site developers to fund salaries, initial auction prizes, advertising, general and administrative expenses, and hosting costs.
    • 5% is distributed pro-rata to accounts staking our contracts.
    • 75% is distributed to TIME token holders.
  • There is no need to stake your TIME tokens, simply owning them will earn you a payout.
  • All payouts are handled automatically by our system. There is no need to claim.
股息支付
  • 每天，网站的利润分配如下：
    • 20％被分发给网站开发者，以资助工资，初始拍卖奖品，广告，一般和管理费用以及托管费用。
    • 5％按比例分配给我们的合约账户。
    • 75％被分配给TIME令牌持有者。
